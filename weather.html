How it works

Cirrus tells you what conditions actually mean — what to wear, when rain arrives, whether to bring an umbrella.

No App Store, no download — add Cirrus directly from your browser and it's there instantly.
Install on iPhone
Open cirrusweather.app in Safari,
then follow these four steps
Tap the ··· button It's in the bottom-right corner of Safari
Tap Share It's right at the top of the menu that appears
Tap Add to Home Screen Scroll down a little — it has a ⊕ icon next to it
Tap the blue Add button It's in the top-right corner — and you're done!
Install on Android
Open cirrusweather.app in Chrome,
then follow these steps
Tap the ⋮ menu button Three dots in the top-right corner of Chrome
Tap Add to Home screen You may see "Install app" instead — either one works
Tap Add or Install to confirm Cirrus appears on your home screen instantly
💡 Chrome may also show an Install banner at the bottom of the screen automatically — just tap that instead!
